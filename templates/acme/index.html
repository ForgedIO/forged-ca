How validation works

When a client asks for a cert for host.example, step-ca proves the client actually owns host.example before signing. Pick whichever challenges your environment can answer:

  • HTTP-01 · step-ca fetches http://host.example/.well-known/acme-challenge/<token> and expects a specific response. Needs inbound port 80 reachable from this node.
  • TLS-ALPN-01 · step-ca opens a TLS connection to host.example:443 with the acme-tls/1 ALPN protocol and reads the challenge from the handshake. Needs inbound port 443 and a client that speaks ALPN.
  • DNS-01 · step-ca checks _acme-challenge.host.example TXT. Needs a DNS-provider plugin to publish the record programmatically — plugin UI is a later slice.
