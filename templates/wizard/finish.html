Why we're not signing this Web UI yet.
Leaf certs (like this admin UI) should come from an Issuing CA. Signing them directly off a Root or Intermediate uses those keys online in a way that undoes the whole reason for tiering. Stand up an Issuing CA on a separate host, federate it to this node, and sign the Web UI off that.

Below you'll still grab the Root so your workstations and fleet trust everything your CA eventually signs — that setup pays off the moment your Issuing CA comes online.
